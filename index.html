Lunar Shadows" (Fantasy/Adventure)

Synopsis: You play as a warrior lost in a mystical forest haunted by dark forces under the glow of the moon. Guided by mysterious beings, you must defeat shadowy enemies, solve ancient puzzles, and navigate the labyrinthine woods to recover a magical artifact that can lift the curse plaguing the land.

"Curse of Hollow Manor" (Horror)

Synopsis: In this eerie game, players explore an abandoned mansion where supernatural occurrences abound. You must unravel the dark history of the manor, encountering vengeful spirits and solving cryptic clues before you're consumed by the house's malevolent forces. Expect jump scares, chilling atmospheres, and psychological horror.

"Skyfall Races" (Racing)

Synopsis: Set in a world of floating islands, you take part in high-speed air races using winged creatures and fantasy vehicles. Compete against others through perilous sky courses, dodging obstacles like storms and magical barriers. Customize your racer and climb the ranks to become the ultimate champion of the skies.
